PT Milenial Canggu Kapital is an investment holdings company headquartered in Indonesia. We build, acquire, and grow companies that serve the rapidly expanding Indonesian middle class.

Our portfolio spans automotive commerce, fresh food marketplaces, and luxury hospitality — sectors where Indonesia's growth story is most compelling. We bring operational excellence, technology, and long-term capital to every venture we back.
